## Songwriting Checklist

Before generating in Suno, verify:

- [ ] Every section has a structural tag
- [ ] Performance cues are included in tags where dynamics matter
- [ ] Lines are short (6-10 syllables)
- [ ] Syllable counts are roughly consistent within sections
- [ ] The chorus is simple and repeatable
- [ ] Complex words are avoided in the chorus/hook
- [ ] The emotional arc builds through the song
- [ ] The lyrics read well when spoken aloud

## Section-by-Section Notes

| Section | Purpose | Tips |
|---------|---------|------|
| **Intro** | Set the scene | Keep short (4-8 bars). Instrumental or minimal vocal. |
| **Verse** | Tell the story | New information each verse. Concrete imagery. |
| **Pre-Chorus** | Build tension | Shorter than verse. Creates anticipation for chorus. |
| **Chorus** | Emotional thesis | Simple, repetitive, singable. This is what people remember. |
| **Bridge** | Departure | New perspective, different energy. Usually appears once. |
| **Outro** | Close it | Resolve, fade, or recontextualize. Do not introduce new ideas. |
