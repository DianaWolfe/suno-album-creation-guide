## Genre Stack Reference

| Mood | Suggested Stack |
|------|----------------|
| Dark and atmospheric | `gothic, lo-fi, atmospheric` |
| Bright and energetic | `uptempo, bright, driving` |
| Intimate and stripped | `acoustic, minimal, intimate` |
| Heavy and raw | `raw, distorted, gritty` |
| Dreamy and spacious | `dreamy, reverb-heavy, ethereal` |
| Soulful and warm | `soulful, warm, analog` |

## Vocal Descriptor Reference

| Quality | Options |
|---------|---------|
| Gender | female, male, androgynous |
| Register | soprano, alto, tenor, baritone, bass |
| Texture | raspy, breathy, clear, gravelly, smooth, airy, rich |
| Delivery | understated, defiant, intimate, full-throated, whispered, conversational |

## Production Descriptor Reference

| Quality | Options |
|---------|---------|
| Fidelity | lo-fi, polished, raw, radio-ready, garage, analog, digital |
| Space | reverb-heavy, dry, wide stereo, intimate, cavernous, close-miked |
| Energy | late-night, energetic, building, sparse, cinematic, driving, laid-back |
| Era | vintage, modern, retro, classic, contemporary |
